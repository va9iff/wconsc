Welcome

9thWorld Conference of Soft Computing (9thWConSC’24) will be held at the Institute of Control System Azerbaijan National Academy of Sciences in Baku, Azerbaijan, on June 03-05, 2024. Azerbaijan is a country with a 10 million population and a big reputation. Azerbaijan is a miraculous country with its unlimited natural resources, centuries-old culture, history and ancient people, whose lifestyle presents a unique and harmonious combination of the traditions and ceremonies of different cultures and civilizations, you are welcome here.

Baku is the capital and largest city of Azerbaijan, as well as the largest city on Caspian Sea and of the Caucasus region. Baku is the city of the scientific, cultural and industrial center of Azerbaijan. In recent years, Baku has become an important venue for international events. It hosted the 57th Eurovision Song Contest in 2012, the 2015 European Games, the 2016 European Grand Prix, 4th Islamic Solidarity Games and Azerbaijan Grand Prix in 2017, and hosted UEFA Euro 2020.

The main objective of 9th WConSC’24 is to present the latest research and results of scientists related to Soft Computing, Fuzzy Logic & Fuzzy Sets, Uncertainty in Cognition, Graphical Models, Knowledge Acquisition, Information Fusion and Aggregation, Knowledge Representation, Ontologies, Decision Support, Cognitive Modeling, Game Theory, Multi-Criteria Decision Making, Granular Computing, Computing with Words, Human-Centric Computing, Rough Sets, Evolutionary Computation, Neural Networks, Hybrid Architectures.

9th World Conference on Soft Computing, Baku 2024, aims to bring together researchers, engineers and practitioners to present the latest achievements and innovations in the multiple areas of soft computing, to discuss thought-provoking developments and challenges, to consider potential future directions. One of the objectives of the conference is to encourage participation by young researchers; to this end student papers will be given a special consideration.

The organizing committee of conference is pleased to invite prospective authors to submit their original manuscripts to 9thWConSC’24. All full paper submissions will be peer reviewed and evaluated based on originality, technical and/or research content/depth, correctness, relevance to conference, contributions, and readability.

Selected papers presented in the conference that match with the topics of the journals will be published in the following journals and Springer publication:

• Springer publication
• International Journal of Applied and Computational Mathematics, (IJ ACM)
• TWMS Pure and Applied Mathematics
